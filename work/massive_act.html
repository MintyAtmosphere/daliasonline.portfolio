Massive Act is a startup that aims to disrupt the live music industry. By allowing artists to create accounts and connect with live music venues, Massive Act hopes to eliminate existing barriers artists face to help them start touring and making money.





At Massive Act, I was tasked with refreshing the marketing website filled with rebranded illustrations and new content. While wire-framing the concept for the new site, I did some research to find what could be improved on the current website.





Aside from the website redesign, I also worked with the team on possible solutions for some other problems we found as we researched improvements for the site, and created a prototype for the mobile experience of the platform and designed posters for the platforms first California tour.
