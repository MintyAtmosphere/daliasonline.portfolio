Casa de Chocolates is a chocolate shop in Berkeley, California. They specialize in Mexican Chocolates and Desserts.

You can shop on their website casadechocolates.com

Our task at Casa de Chocolates was to create a more modern, user-friendly site that integrated seamlessly with social media and that would attract more customers. I worked with a product manager on creating a marketing website that would generate more revenue, and be easier to use and update for the client. Aside from designing the new site, I migrated the shop to Shopify from WooCommerce.

I also worked on a style guide for the shop to continue using for social media marketing.
